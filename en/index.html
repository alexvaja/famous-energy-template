About our Company

FAMOUS ENERGY LIVE STREAM

Welcome to FAMOUS ENERGY LIVE STREAM - your trusted partner in the field of solar energy! With long-term experience in the field, we have dedicated ourselves to providing innovative solutions and sustainable in the field of renewable energy. We specialize in the sale of equipment and accessories high-quality photovoltaics, while providing professional warranty and post-warranty services for batteries and inverters.

Why FAMOUS ENERGY LIVE STREAM?

  • Superior quality: We are committed to bringing only the highest quality PV equipment to market. Our products are carefully selected to ensure long-term performance and durability.
  • Extended Warranty: At FAMOUS ENERGY LIVE STREAM, we are dedicated to our customers' satisfaction. We offer warranty services extended for batteries and inverters, ensuring that your solar power system is always protected and operating at optimal parameters.
  • Expert advice: Our team of solar experts are here to advise you specialized and customized solutions. Whether you are a home owner, a business or a institution, we can help you find the most suitable solution for your specific needs.
  • Commitment to the environment: We are proud to help protect the environment through promotion solar energy. By using photovoltaic equipment, our customers contribute to reducing their footprint of carbon and supporting a more sustainable future.

Our company can offer you the following consulting services for your project:

  • Consultancy of renewable energy projects
  • Consultancy for authorisation, financing, sale of projects, construction, commissioning, operation and maintenance of capacities energy as well as the sale of the produced energy
  • Coordinator of renewable energy projects

Within our renewable energy capacity implementation projects and as a manager of energy projects renewable, we undertook:

  • Authorization and implementation of photovoltaic and wind power plants
  • Verification and control of construction and network connection works
  • Follow-up and compliance with the deadlines for putting energy capacities into operation
  • Preliminary studies / Feasibility studies for renewable energy projects
  • Identification of financing sources for renewable energy projects
  • Preparation of documentation for project financing
  • Preparation of the documentation for obtaining the connection to the network
  • Obtaining construction permits and approvals
  • Preparation of documentation and obtaining the license for the exploitation of energy capacities
  • Collaboration with economic, technical and legal departments in order to analyze documents, from an economic and technical point of view and legal for the financing of energy projects
  • Coordinating the teams in order to build and put the projects into operation at the set deadline
  • Identifying and solving problems arising in the authorization, implementation and operation of projects energy
  • Coordinator of projects and teams in order to build and put projects into operation at the set deadline

Projects authorized and put into operation:

  • Mihai Viteazu wind farm - 5.6 MW - Constanta county
  • Pantelimon wind farm - 4 MW - Constanta county
  • Photovoltaic park Galbenu - 2.5 MW - Braila county
  • Photovoltaic park Traian - 2.5 MW - Braila county

Projects authorized for construction:
